How Secure is my data on Fisco?

Your data on Fisco is highly secure, protected by advanced encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits to ensure maximum safety.

Can I link my bank accounts to Fisco?

Yes, you can link your bank accounts to Fisco securely, enabling seamless financial management and monitoring.

How do I set financial goals in Fisco?

To set financial goals in Fisco, navigate to the "Goals" section, click "Create New Goal," specify your goal details (such as amount, duration, and purpose), and then save your goal. Fisco will help you track your progress and stay on target.
